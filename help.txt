Comando         Argumentos                  Descrição
------------------------------------------------------
SEND            <MESSAGE>                   Send <MESSAGE> to all conected clients.
SENDTO          <CLIENT_NAME> <MESSAGE>     Send message only to specific client by <CLIENT_NAME>.
NAME		<CLIENT_NAME>		    Change the client name.
WHO              ---                        Show list of conected client on server.
HELP             ---                        Show list of command.
EXIT             ---                        Terminate the client program.
